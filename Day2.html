Taylor Swift

What to even say about Miss Americana, she's one of a kind. Wide variety of music spanning multiple genres, with genius songwriting, beats you get hooked on, topped with amazing vocals.... She has songs for every occasion and moods!


From the age of 14, she perused her career in country music. Releasing her first single, Tim McGraw Swift began her journey in the music industry in 2006. Releasing the self titled debut album, followed Fearless and Speak Now. She then experimented with pop in some songs on her next album, Red. 1989 came after that. Hit Singles such as Bad blood and Shake it off could be heard on every radio.


Due to some controversies, she took a break from the music industry and focussed on herself. She came back stronger than a 90's trend!


Reputation album was one the greatest comeback of all time! All her posts from her social media were deleted, symbolising a fresh start. She did this before also before releasing 1989, her first complete pop album, she cut her hair, marking her transition from country sweetheart to a Pop star. She embraced the hate and turned it into the amazing album it is. The reputation tour marked Swift's most successful tour to-date, and broke many records, such as becoming the highest-grossing tour ever in the United States and North America.


Right now, releasing indie folk music in albums such as Folklore and Evermore she is showing off her extreme versatility. She is also re recording her past albums.


Taylor Swift is a very inspiring icon. She always puts her best foot forward despite the situations. She turns adverses situations into opportunities. She is very talented and makes great music. I have listened to her music a lot and look upto her as a role model. I look forward to her upcoming projects and I'm sure she will continue to surprise us!
